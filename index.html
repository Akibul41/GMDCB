NEP 2020

The National Education Policy 2020 (NEP 2020) heralds a transformative era in India's educational landscape. Envisioned to revamp the outdated system, NEP 2020 emphasizes holistic development, flexibility, and innovation. With a focus on early childhood education, vocational training, and multidisciplinary learning, it aims to nurture well-rounded individuals equipped for the challenges of tomorrow. Embracing technology, language diversity, and experiential learning, NEP 2020 fosters inclusivity and equitable access to quality education across socio-economic strata. By encouraging critical thinking and creativity, it aspires to catalyze India's journey towards becoming a global knowledge hub, empowering its youth to lead with confidence and competence in the 21st century.
